Monthly Auto Detailing Agreement

This agreement is between Jaguar Jet Wash and the Customer

for monthly auto detailing services.

1. Service Plan

  • Customer has selected the plan indicated above.
  • Services are provided as outlined in the selected plan description.
  • All services are scheduled by appointment only.

2. Service Frequency

  • Services will be provided as outlined in the selected plan.
  • All services are scheduled by appointment only.

3. Payment Terms

  • Monthly fee must be paid in advance.
  • Payment is due on or before the start of each month.
  • No refunds for missed or unused visits.

4. Term of Agreement

  • Minimum commitment: 3 months.
  • Agreement renews monthly unless cancelled with 7 days notice.

5. Conditions

  • Plan covers one vehicle only.
  • Excessive dirt, pet hair, sand, or stains may result in additional charges.
  • Missed appointments do not roll over.
  • Jaguar Jet Wash is not responsible for pre-existing damage.

6. Cancellation

  • Either party may cancel after the minimum term with written notice.
